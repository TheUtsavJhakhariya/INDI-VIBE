Founded in Mumbai by visionary artists, INDI VIBE is a dynamic platform dedicated to nurturing and showcasing the finest talents in Bollywood music and Indian rap.

About Us

Welcome to INDI VIBE — the heartbeat of Bollywood music and Indian rap culture! Since 2010, we've been redefining the soundscape of India, bringing together the best of rhythm, rhyme, and raw energy. From electrifying concerts in packed arenas to chart-topping releases that dominate the airwaves, we celebrate the vibrant fusion of tradition and modern beats. Our journey has featured iconic performances by legends like Yo Yo Honey Singh and Badshah, while also giving a stage to rising stars like DIVINE and KR$NA. At INDI VIBE, we’re not just about music — we’re about moments. Moments that bring people together, fuel passion, and create memories. Join us as we continue to push the boundaries, hosting unforgettable events and showcasing talent that shapes the future of Bollywood and Indian rap.
